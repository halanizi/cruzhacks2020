#3 Polyvinyl Chloride

Found in shampoo bottles, medical plastics, some toys (including dog) and window trim, this plastic is typically not used for household items that can be consumed as it can contain phthalates.

PVC is usually recycled into paneling, flooring, cables and decks. Most items made from this plastic cannot be recycled, though any PVC plastic containers with the recycling logo can be recycled in your curbside recycling containers.
