#2 High Density Polyethylene

This kind of plastic is usually colored or opaque and can be found in milk jugs, detergent and other household cleaner bottles. HDPE can easily be recycled into plastic lumber, more bottles or drainage pipes and is considered one of the safest forms of plastic.

This type of plastic is also accepted by curbside recycling programs and cashback recycling programs, though plastic grocery bags are the exception, and are not accepted by most recycling programs of any type.
