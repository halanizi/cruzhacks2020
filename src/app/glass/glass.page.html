Glass

Everything You Need to Know About Glass Recycling

Good news!

Glass is 100% Recyclable! Since glass comes from domestic materials (sand, stone, cullet), it can be endlessly recycled without loss in quality. Items including:

  • Glass Bottles
  • Glass Jars
  • Glass Containers

are all qualifying recyclable items. Not only will recycling reduce the waste entering our landfill, but it will also reduce the emissions generated from manufacturing plants, which benefits our evironment even more over the long run.

Unfortunately, only 33% of the average 10 million metric tons of glass are recycled annually in the United States, with the rest ending up in landfills. To help out recycle locations, try to avoid broken glass as this makes it more hazardous for facilities to handle. Glass is 100% recyclable, so do your part and put those glass items in recycling bins!
