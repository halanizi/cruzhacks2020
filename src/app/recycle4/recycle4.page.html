#4 Low Density Polyethylene

Bottles you can squeeze like shampoo or condiment bottles are made from LDPE, along with plastic bread bags, six pack rings, and dry cleaning bags. While a lot of recycling centers generally do not accept this type of plastic, any LDPE plastic containers with the recycling symbol can be recycled using your curbside recycling program.

LDPE can be recycled into more bags or trash liners and floor tiles.
